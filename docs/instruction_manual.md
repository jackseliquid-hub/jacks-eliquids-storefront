## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Kitchen
The "Kitchen" is the administrative area of your website. Only you and your staff can see it.
1. Go to your website's login page.
2. Log in with your admin email and password.
3. Once logged in, click the **My Account** button, or go directly to `yourwebsite.com/admin`.

---

## 2. The Kitchen Dashboard

When you open the Kitchen, you'll see a navigation menu on the left side. This is your main toolkit.

- **Dashboard:** A quick overview of your sales and recent activity.
- **Product Editors:** Everything related to what you sell (Products, Categories, Brands).
- **Orders:** Where you manage customer purchases.
- **Customers:** A list of people who have created accounts.
- **Storefront:** Tools to change how the website looks (Banners, Menus, Media).
- **Marketing:** Reviews, Blogs, SEO, and Discounts.

---

## 3. Managing Products

This section is where you add new vape juices, kits, and coils.

### Adding or Editing a Product
1. Click **Products** in the left menu, then either click **＋ Add Product** or the **Edit** button next to an existing product.
2. Fill in or update the basic details (Name, Price, Images).
3. **Use AI to Write Your Content!**
   There are three places where the **✨ Generate with AI** button can do the hard work for you:
   - **Short Description:** Click the AI button to write a quick, catchy summary (under 100 words).
   - **Long Description:** Click the AI button to write a detailed, full-page explanation of the product's features.
   - **SEO Details:** Scroll to the bottom SEO card and click the AI button to automatically write Google-friendly Titles and Meta Descriptions.
4. **Categories & Brands:** Select which category (e.g., E-Liquid, Hardware) and Brand it belongs to.
5. Click **Save Product**.

*Quick Edit Tip:* You can click directly on prices, stock, or categories in the main product list to change them quickly without opening the full edit page.

### Categories, Brands, and Tags
If you need a new Category (e.g., "Nic Salts") or Brand:
- Click **Categories** or **Brands** in the menu to add them.
- You can also add these directly while editing a product by clicking the **＋ Add New** button next to the category dropdown.

## 4. Managing Orders

When a customer buys something, it appears here.

### Processing an Order
1. Click **Orders** in the left menu.
2. You will see a list of recent orders. Click **View** on an order to see what they bought and their address.
3. **Change Status:** Once you have packed the box and posted it, change the order status to **Shipped**. 
4. The system will automatically email the customer to tell them their order is on the way!

---

## 5. Homepage Promotions & Banners

You can easily change the large images and special offers on your homepage.

1. Click **Homepage Promos** (or Banners) in the left menu.
2. You have three tabs here:
   - **Hero Banners:** The large, sliding images at the very top of the homepage.
     - *Tip:* Use images sized 1920 × 600px.
   - **Promo Tiles:** The smaller rectangular or circular call-out boxes.
     - You can drag and drop these to reorder them!
   - **Showcases:** The rows of featured products (e.g., "Top Selling E-Liquids").
3. To change any of these, click **✏️ Edit**, change the text or image, and click **Save**.

---

## 6. Managing Customer Reviews

Your website collects reviews directly from your customers.

1. Click **⭐ Reviews** in the left menu.
2. Here you will see two sections: **Pending** and **Published**.
3. **Pending Reviews:** When a customer leaves a new review, it stays hidden until you approve it.
   - Click **✓ Publish** to show it on the homepage.
   - Click **🗑 Delete** if it is spam.
4. **💬 Reply to Customer:** You can click the Reply button to send a personal thank-you email directly to the customer.

---

## 7. Blogs & Pages

Keeping your site updated with news helps with Google rankings.

### Writing a Blog Post
1. Click **📝 Blogs** in the left menu.
2. Click **New Post**.
3. Add a Title, an Image, and write your article.
4. Click **Publish**.

### Editing Pages
1. Click **📄 Pages**.
2. Here you can edit the text for your "About Us", "Contact", or "Terms & Conditions" pages.

---

## 8. Discounts & Coupons

Want to run a sale or give out a promo code?

1. Click **🏷️ Coupon Codes** or **💰 Discounts**.
2. Click **New Code**.
3. Choose the code name (e.g., `SUMMER20`).
4. Decide if it's a percentage off (e.g., 20% off) or a flat amount (e.g., £5 off).
5. Set an expiry date if needed, and click **Save**.

---

## 9. Media Library

This is where all the photos for your website are stored.

1. Click **🖼️ Media Library**.
2. You can upload new images here, or delete old ones you no longer need.
3. Whenever you add an image to a product or banner, you will select it from this library.

---

## 10. SEO (Getting Found on Google)

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It's how you tell Google what your website is about.

1. Click **🔍 SEO** in the left menu.
2. You will see a list of your pages and products.
3. You can edit the **Title** and **Description** that appears on Google search results.
4. *Tip:* Use the **✨ AI Generate** button! The system will read your product details and automatically write a perfect, Google-friendly description for you.
